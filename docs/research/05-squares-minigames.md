| **Shop** | Buy if unowned; pay the fee if an opponent's; invest (any of your shops, ≤999G/turn) if yours |
| **Vacant plot** | Standard rules: build one of 7 buildings (see 02) |

## Arcade minigames

**Round the Blocks** — slot machine. Line up three-of-a-kind:
- three 7s: **500G × your level**
- three mushrooms/slimes: 50G × level
- three warp symbols: teleport
- three stock icons: stocks (Standard only)
- Suit Yourself line: a Suit Yourself card

**Memory Block** — pick boxes from a grid, remembering reveals:
- 1-Up: all your shops +10%
- coin: 10G × level
- 5 stocks; Suit Yourself card
- Bowser: all your shops −5%

**Dart of Gold** — throw a dart at a prize wheel, then **choose which player
receives the result** (prize or penalty):
- treasure chest 100G × level · coin 10G × level · 3 coins 30G × level
- stocks · 1-Up (+5% shops) · Bowser (−5%) · Suit Yourself

**Slurpodrome** — slime racing. Pick a slime; racers collect chests on the
track (coin 20G · Mimic eliminates the racer · Munchie nothing · Seed speed
boost). Winner: **100G × level** + coins collected. Under Easy rules rewards
are doubled.

## Suit Yourself cards

Wildcards redeemable as any missing suit at promotion. Earned from venture
cards (39/89/100/106/124) and minigames. **Hard cap: 9 per player.** A suit
freebie you already own converts to 100G.

## Cameo characters (from venture cards)

Wandering NPCs that act on their own each round: **goodybag** (pays fees at
shops it lands on), **healslime** (grants a suit when you pass it),
**Lakitu** (closes shops it lands on). **[S]**
