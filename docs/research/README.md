# Fortune Street — Original Rules Research

Research into the mechanics of *Fortune Street* (Wii, 2011 — *Boom Street* in
PAL, *Itadaki Street Wii* in Japan), conducted 2026-06-11 to ground Bankrupt
Street's house rules in an accurate picture of the original.

## Confidence legend

- **[V]** Verified — multiple sources agree, or derived empirically from the
  Fortune Street Modding district simulator / decompilation-backed tools.
- **[S]** Single source — one reliable source, not cross-checked.
- **[U]** Uncertain — sources conflict, or community recollection.
